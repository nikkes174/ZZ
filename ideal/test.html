Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your bánh mì different?

We focus on authentic Northern Vietnamese flavours, which are lighter, more balanced, and less sweet. Everything is built around fresh ingredients and bread baked daily at 5am!

Is your bread really baked fresh every day?

Yes! Our bread is baked fresh every morning at 5am. That’s what gives our bánh mì its crispiness on the outside and the fluffiness on the inside.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes, we offer convenient Indian takeaway and Nepalese delivery in Coventry. Order online or call us.
